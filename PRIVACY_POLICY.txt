PRIVACY POLICY FOR VOIKY

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

1. OVERVIEW
VoiKy is a voice typing application developed by neowist. This privacy policy explains how VoiKy handles your data.

2. DATA COLLECTION BY VOIKY
VoiKy itself does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to neowist or any third parties. Specifically:
- No user information is collected by VoiKy
- No analytics or telemetry data is gathered by VoiKy
- No typed text is stored or transmitted by VoiKy

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES - GOOGLE WEB SPEECH API
VoiKy uses Google's Web Speech API to perform voice recognition. When you use VoiKy:
- Audio from your microphone IS transmitted to Google's servers over the internet
- Google processes the audio to convert speech to text
- Google's privacy policy and terms of service apply to this data transmission
- An internet connection IS REQUIRED for VoiKy to function

For information about how Google handles your data, please review:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Cloud Speech-to-Text Terms: https://cloud.google.com/terms

4. MICROPHONE ACCESS
VoiKy requires access to your microphone to perform voice recognition. Audio captured from your microphone is:
- Transmitted to Google's servers for speech recognition processing
- Processed according to Google's privacy policy
- Not stored or accessed by VoiKy or neowist

5. DATA STORAGE
VoiKy does not create, maintain, or access any databases or files containing user data. The application only stores:
- Application settings and preferences locally on your device
- No personal or sensitive information

6. DATA TRANSMITTED
The only data transmitted by VoiKy is:
- Audio data sent to Google Web Speech API for speech recognition
- No other data is transmitted to any servers

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
VoiKy does not knowingly collect any information from children. However, audio data is transmitted to Google's services, which have their own policies regarding children's data.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the application and on our distribution channels.
